A space for prompts related to planning, brainstorming, and task management.
### **Task & Project Management**

- Break down a large project, such as "plan a family vacation," into a step-by-step checklist.

- Generate a list of potential obstacles for a new business venture and brainstorm solutions for each.

### **Brainstorming**

- Create a mind map of ideas for a new blog post series.

- Come up with a list of 10 ways to improve work-life balance.

### **Personal Growth**

- Write a prompt to help you reflect on your goals for the coming year.
  
- Generate a daily morning routine to boost productivity.
