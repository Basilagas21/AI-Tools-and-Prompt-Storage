A space for inspiring stories, artistic ideas, and imaginative concepts.

### **Story Ideas**

- **Character Concept:** A librarian who can hear the whispers of books. They can tell you which ones are lonely or which ones want to be read.
    
- **Plot Twist:** A seemingly heroic character is actually the one pulling all the strings for their own benefit.
    
- **Setting:** A floating city powered by captured lightning and inhabited by a society that reveres storms.
    

### **Poetry Prompts**

- Write a poem from the perspective of an old tree in a busy park.
    
- Use a single color as the central theme for a poem about a memory.
    

### **Artistic Prompts**

- A surreal landscape where the sky and the ground are interchangeable.
    
- Design a new mythical creature inspired by a common household pet.
