A space for prompts related to learning, teaching, and summarization
### **Summarization**

- Create a list of key takeaways from a long lecture or podcast on a historical event.
    

### **Study Guides & Explanations**

- Generate a study guide for an upcoming test on the periodic table.
    
- Explain the concept of photosynthesis using a simple analogy.
    

### **Lesson Planning**

- Create a series of prompts for a discussion group on the themes of a classic
