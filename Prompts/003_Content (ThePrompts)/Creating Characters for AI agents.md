The design style can be described as:

- **Minimalist Line Art:** The core of the face (eyes and nose) is created with simple black lines.
    
- **Abstract/Geometric:** The face is not a realistic representation but an abstract arrangement of simple shapes. The eyes are small circles, the nose is a bent line, and the eyebrows/glasses are curved or straight lines.
    
- **Modular/Component-based:** The design is built from a few interchangeable components. The eyes and nose are static, while the "accessories" like eyebrows, glasses, or hats change to represent different agent personalities. This modularity makes it easy to create variations.
    
- **"Human-like" but not Human:** The design evokes a human face without being detailed or lifelike, which makes it friendly and approachable.
