## Prompt
Imagine i dont know anything about AI agents, what kind of efficient and core roadmap would you suggest to me?

## Results

It's exciting that you're looking to learn about AI agents! They represent a significant leap in artificial intelligence, enabling systems to act more autonomously and intelligently. Since you're starting from scratch, here’s an efficient and core roadmap designed to build your understanding step-by-step:

### What are AI Agents?

At its most fundamental, an AI agent is a software system that uses artificial intelligence to perceive its environment, make decisions, and take actions to achieve specific goals on behalf of a user[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)][[2](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQF6nD4O3ooYEuX0JM3h-4qLKuyycqeHjNaLeELXsnkQ3yUuRr3apiskxDHSyTCkHbVb-q8Ecgh-zD34IkpMksl-vkfaufJjp6513ALKc0lgxlUD6ZUWOVVnst2epfDUwCkYhApDbmYuSNeWC5wzd1gGx5CjvQ%3D%3D)][[3](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQEszqUuVGsAFydbDWm09hjLZKKT9DQldCdrKdCt3bMMVyao93fuBweXPKex0hxDAtYPy02Lnq0SuvIbAx5uLA-_NFHjGnOb4qs4LLgYAPW9JVjZCkmE_981AtwI)]. Unlike traditional software that follows rigid, pre-programmed instructions, AI agents can make dynamic decisions, learn, and adapt over time[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)][[2](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQF6nD4O3ooYEuX0JM3h-4qLKuyycqeHjNaLeELXsnkQ3yUuRr3apiskxDHSyTCkHbVb-q8Ecgh-zD34IkpMksl-vkfaufJjp6513ALKc0lgxlUD6ZUWOVVnst2epfDUwCkYhApDbmYuSNeWC5wzd1gGx5CjvQ%3D%3D)]. They often exhibit reasoning, planning, and memory capabilities[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)].

**Key Differences from Traditional AI/Bots:**

- **Autonomy:** AI agents have a high degree of independence, operating and making decisions without constant human intervention to achieve a goal[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)][[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGYqgrGZW76OCMxCtZzwapwrFQnAMxd6XElxwFED96_uH7KSzTdMC9LgdDLVj0h5dtzuBZd6SXAgJ5YDcDu53sUkH9RUckQwVWF3mnXhG7g5QYxsi34KHwNIXRDNQryRjkuP8g%3D)]. Bots, for instance, are typically less autonomous and follow pre-programmed rules, while AI assistants require more user input and direction[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)].
    
- **Complexity:** Agents are designed for complex tasks and workflows, whereas bots are better suited for simpler, often repetitive tasks[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)].
    
- **Learning:** Many AI agents employ machine learning to adapt and improve their performance through experience, differentiating them from static programs[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)][[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGYqgrGZW76OCMxCtZzwapwrFQnAMxd6XElxwFED96_uH7KSzTdMC9LgdDLVj0h5dtzuBZd6SXAgJ5YDcDu53sUkH9RUckQwVWF3mnXhG7g5QYxsi34KHwNIXRDNQryRjkuP8g%3D)].
    

### Core Components of an AI Agent

An AI agent typically consists of several key components that work together in a continuous cycle of perception, thought, and action[[2](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQF6nD4O3ooYEuX0JM3h-4qLKuyycqeHjNaLeELXsnkQ3yUuRr3apiskxDHSyTCkHbVb-q8Ecgh-zD34IkpMksl-vkfaufJjp6513ALKc0lgxlUD6ZUWOVVnst2epfDUwCkYhApDbmYuSNeWC5wzd1gGx5CjvQ%3D%3D)][[5](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFf30QZR3bkdvUury3RwX9smB82Oxv10epGXZRdS69W4Ge3kn2wwG7K5h0hS8ZL-3TWFkuIjrtTae8cW6VUsQRU1sJtM8kSj5NxtdNxLrJKEfmTiCEgssmvNTr8TMFmhNEeINAqY5-vfQ%3D%3D)]:

1. **Perception Module (Sensors/Input):** This allows the agent to gather information from its environment. This can include digital inputs (like text, voice, video, code, data streams, APIs) or physical sensors in the case of embodied AI (like robots)[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)][[3](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQEszqUuVGsAFydbDWm09hjLZKKT9DQldCdrKdCt3bMMVyao93fuBweXPKex0hxDAtYPy02Lnq0SuvIbAx5uLA-_NFHjGnOb4qs4LLgYAPW9JVjZCkmE_981AtwI)][[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGYqgrGZW76OCMxCtZzwapwrFQnAMxd6XElxwFED96_uH7KSzTdMC9LgdDLVj0h5dtzuBZd6SXAgJ5YDcDu53sUkH9RUckQwVWF3mnXhG7g5QYxsi34KHwNIXRDNQryRjkuP8g%3D)][[5](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFf30QZR3bkdvUury3RwX9smB82Oxv10epGXZRdS69W4Ge3kn2wwG7K5h0hS8ZL-3TWFkuIjrtTae8cW6VUsQRU1sJtM8kSj5NxtdNxLrJKEfmTiCEgssmvNTr8TMFmhNEeINAqY5-vfQ%3D%3D)].
    
2. **Model (The Brain/LLM):** At the core of many modern AI agents is a Large Language Model (LLM) or other foundation models. This acts as the "brain," enabling the agent to understand natural language inputs, reason, generate human-like responses, and process complex instructions[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)][[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGYqgrGZW76OCMxCtZzwapwrFQnAMxd6XElxwFED96_uH7KSzTdMC9LgdDLVj0h5dtzuBZd6SXAgJ5YDcDu53sUkH9RUckQwVWF3mnXhG7g5QYxsi34KHwNIXRDNQryRjkuP8g%3D)][[6](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFloFNfjjsgu_kpbDpYFfsILx6W1kam_0mHTlBAcM-TMZGkuwvWDIXD2XKQzRt1ttOoqkBtwbbVO9eh-QojqdYjNTuB_hdaBkR9LH19faut4Yta8U8iGO4i8wYvEceH1vI_Ar1xKcQ6)][[7](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQG55XJ2bYua_f75CpSvcxg0Bzel1qTYyUj5hlHKYwWpHynAekkqnPi4O93COKRgrJgkzdN0MjVHPm7vwAMS28oqWhj1N6W0GP6d_Jh3ZXJVIMWatEhZQyQXTk5bql9wMNd6O6GJYGgmGVP7dckg9abiOK4HlT4GRXuRjCa6pvACy7e7yQW7204hClfJoA5mJ0QlYRPHpywm)].
    
3. **Decision-Making/Planning Module:** Based on its perceptions and understanding from the model, this module determines the next steps. It involves using logic, planning strategic actions to achieve goals, and often continuously reassessing and self-correcting its plan[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)][[5](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFf30QZR3bkdvUury3RwX9smB82Oxv10epGXZRdS69W4Ge3kn2wwG7K5h0hS8ZL-3TWFkuIjrtTae8cW6VUsQRU1sJtM8kSj5NxtdNxLrJKEfmTiCEgssmvNTr8TMFmhNEeINAqY5-vfQ%3D%3D)][[8](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQHPbKVVvEVGp6NgleRTZ0rxmq2R64Pkz_6EKh85LagsHjpYAQ2aloxNURaUGHQJwf5t8OFe2qvEcMEzagQYHrWA5L9to1_L8VbM6JxdnZ88jNplVahDq80ri1qQvcQhLwvU-2-8)][[9](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGv6b5V0WrbdgEtzlGJRXOQTF91C2YEcGOLS6n5lf1KzIewUIM5vFBUGgat5UJIrEbDwvfp6rjxRDsCyixGMHWZiTJ6f2xyptE_LOa2j03DLQCwIjGp4EmYneYWHU-WnmE2DH7YRxytYmfJ18Dd_lV_NdogO5HotQhd-fDeXK81-xqNoT1uVPavJcz7hb6JNw%3D%3D)].
    
4. **Action Module (Actuators/Tools):** This component enables the agent to interact with its environment by performing tasks or taking actions. This can involve digital actions (sending messages, updating data, using external APIs, executing code) or physical actions (for robots)[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)][[3](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQEszqUuVGsAFydbDWm09hjLZKKT9DQldCdrKdCt3bMMVyao93fuBweXPKex0hxDAtYPy02Lnq0SuvIbAx5uLA-_NFHjGnOb4qs4LLgYAPW9JVjZCkmE_981AtwI)][[5](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFf30QZR3bkdvUury3RwX9smB82Oxv10epGXZRdS69W4Ge3kn2wwG7K5h0hS8ZL-3TWFkuIjrtTae8cW6VUsQRU1sJtM8kSj5NxtdNxLrJKEfmTiCEgssmvNTr8TMFmhNEeINAqY5-vfQ%3D%3D)][[7](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQG55XJ2bYua_f75CpSvcxg0Bzel1qTYyUj5hlHKYwWpHynAekkqnPi4O93COKRgrJgkzdN0MjVHPm7vwAMS28oqWhj1N6W0GP6d_Jh3ZXJVIMWatEhZQyQXTk5bql9wMNd6O6GJYGgmGVP7dckg9abiOK4HlT4GRXuRjCa6pvACy7e7yQW7204hClfJoA5mJ0QlYRPHpywm)][[8](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQHPbKVVvEVGp6NgleRTZ0rxmq2R64Pkz_6EKh85LagsHjpYAQ2aloxNURaUGHQJwf5t8OFe2qvEcMEzagQYHrWA5L9to1_L8VbM6JxdnZ88jNplVahDq80ri1qQvcQhLwvU-2-8)].
    
5. **Memory Systems:** Agents often have various forms of memory to maintain context, learn from experiences, and improve performance[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)][[6](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFloFNfjjsgu_kpbDpYFfsILx6W1kam_0mHTlBAcM-TMZGkuwvWDIXD2XKQzRt1ttOoqkBtwbbVO9eh-QojqdYjNTuB_hdaBkR9LH19faut4Yta8U8iGO4i8wYvEceH1vI_Ar1xKcQ6)][[9](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGv6b5V0WrbdgEtzlGJRXOQTF91C2YEcGOLS6n5lf1KzIewUIM5vFBUGgat5UJIrEbDwvfp6rjxRDsCyixGMHWZiTJ6f2xyptE_LOa2j03DLQCwIjGp4EmYneYWHU-WnmE2DH7YRxytYmfJ18Dd_lV_NdogO5HotQhd-fDeXK81-xqNoT1uVPavJcz7hb6JNw%3D%3D)]. This includes:
    
    - **Short-term memory:** For immediate interactions and context within a single conversation[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)][[6](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFloFNfjjsgu_kpbDpYFfsILx6W1kam_0mHTlBAcM-TMZGkuwvWDIXD2XKQzRt1ttOoqkBtwbbVO9eh-QojqdYjNTuB_hdaBkR9LH19faut4Yta8U8iGO4i8wYvEceH1vI_Ar1xKcQ6)].

### How AI Agents Work (The Cycle)

AI agents typically operate in a continuous cycle, mirroring human cognitive processes:

1. **Perceive:** The agent gathers data from its environment through its sensors or digital inputs[[2](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQF6nD4O3ooYEuX0JM3h-4qLKuyycqeHjNaLeELXsnkQ3yUuRr3apiskxDHSyTCkHbVb-q8Ecgh-zD34IkpMksl-vkfaufJjp6513ALKc0lgxlUD6ZUWOVVnst2epfDUwCkYhApDbmYuSNeWC5wzd1gGx5CjvQ%3D%3D)][[5](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFf30QZR3bkdvUury3RwX9smB82Oxv10epGXZRdS69W4Ge3kn2wwG7K5h0hS8ZL-3TWFkuIjrtTae8cW6VUsQRU1sJtM8kSj5NxtdNxLrJKEfmTiCEgssmvNTr8TMFmhNEeINAqY5-vfQ%3D%3D)].
    
2. **Process/Think:** It then uses its model (e.g., LLM) and decision-making capabilities to interpret this information, analyze it against its goals, and determine the best course of action[[2](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQF6nD4O3ooYEuX0JM3h-4qLKuyycqeHjNaLeELXsnkQ3yUuRr3apiskxDHSyTCkHbVb-q8Ecgh-zD34IkpMksl-vkfaufJjp6513ALKc0lgxlUD6ZUWOVVnst2epfDUwCkYhApDbmYuSNeWC5wzd1gGx5CjvQ%3D%3D)][[5](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFf30QZR3bkdvUury3RwX9smB82Oxv10epGXZRdS69W4Ge3kn2wwG7K5h0hS8ZL-3TWFkuIjrtTae8cW6VUsQRU1sJtM8kSj5NxtdNxLrJKEfmTiCEgssmvNTr8TMFmhNEeINAqY5-vfQ%3D%3D)]. This often involves breaking down complex goals into smaller tasks[[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGYqgrGZW76OCMxCtZzwapwrFQnAMxd6XElxwFED96_uH7KSzTdMC9LgdDLVj0h5dtzuBZd6SXAgJ5YDcDu53sUkH9RUckQwVWF3mnXhG7g5QYxsi34KHwNIXRDNQryRjkuP8g%3D)][[8](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQHPbKVVvEVGp6NgleRTZ0rxmq2R64Pkz_6EKh85LagsHjpYAQ2aloxNURaUGHQJwf5t8OFe2qvEcMEzagQYHrWA5L9to1_L8VbM6JxdnZ88jNplVahDq80ri1qQvcQhLwvU-2-8)].
    
3. **Act:** Finally, the agent executes its decision by taking an action, utilizing its tools or actuators[[2](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQF6nD4O3ooYEuX0JM3h-4qLKuyycqeHjNaLeELXsnkQ3yUuRr3apiskxDHSyTCkHbVb-q8Ecgh-zD34IkpMksl-vkfaufJjp6513ALKc0lgxlUD6ZUWOVVnst2epfDUwCkYhApDbmYuSNeWC5wzd1gGx5CjvQ%3D%3D)][[5](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFf30QZR3bkdvUury3RwX9smB82Oxv10epGXZRdS69W4Ge3kn2wwG7K5h0hS8ZL-3TWFkuIjrtTae8cW6VUsQRU1sJtM8kSj5NxtdNxLrJKEfmTiCEgssmvNTr8TMFmhNEeINAqY5-vfQ%3D%3D)].
    
4. **Learn:** Throughout this cycle, especially with more advanced agents, it learns from the outcomes of its actions, adapting and improving its behavior over time[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)][[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGYqgrGZW76OCMxCtZzwapwrFQnAMxd6XElxwFED96_uH7KSzTdMC9LgdDLVj0h5dtzuBZd6SXAgJ5YDcDu53sUkH9RUckQwVWF3mnXhG7g5QYxsi34KHwNIXRDNQryRjkuP8g%3D)][[5](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFf30QZR3bkdvUury3RwX9smB82Oxv10epGXZRdS69W4Ge3kn2wwG7K5h0hS8ZL-3TWFkuIjrtTae8cW6VUsQRU1sJtM8kSj5NxtdNxLrJKEfmTiCEgssmvNTr8TMFmhNEeINAqY5-vfQ%3D%3D)][[9](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGv6b5V0WrbdgEtzlGJRXOQTF91C2YEcGOLS6n5lf1KzIewUIM5vFBUGgat5UJIrEbDwvfp6rjxRDsCyixGMHWZiTJ6f2xyptE_LOa2j03DLQCwIjGp4EmYneYWHU-WnmE2DH7YRxytYmfJ18Dd_lV_NdogO5HotQhd-fDeXK81-xqNoT1uVPavJcz7hb6JNw%3D%3D)].
    

### Types of AI Agents

AI agents can be classified based on their complexity, decision-making processes, and interaction with their environment[[11](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQHkm0jCVQ2NCzgammbuMPWIUE6n_NvI97gtQa9cfeiHVcqDJ4dSbSDVjcnDl16xYewJwYWYPk35fsZEHRb064ffnDkjdhfsYTJ8zj_-3dmTrkgAZ4ew75L1be7C35Fn-rmlN5AUVlpxULE%3D)]. Some common types include:

- **Simple Reflex Agents:** These are the most basic, acting solely based on current perceptions and predefined condition-action rules, without memory of past states or a model of the world[[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGYqgrGZW76OCMxCtZzwapwrFQnAMxd6XElxwFED96_uH7KSzTdMC9LgdDLVj0h5dtzuBZd6SXAgJ5YDcDu53sUkH9RUckQwVWF3mnXhG7g5QYxsi34KHwNIXRDNQryRjkuP8g%3D)][[9](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGv6b5V0WrbdgEtzlGJRXOQTF91C2YEcGOLS6n5lf1KzIewUIM5vFBUGgat5UJIrEbDwvfp6rjxRDsCyixGMHWZiTJ6f2xyptE_LOa2j03DLQCwIjGp4EmYneYWHU-WnmE2DH7YRxytYmfJ18Dd_lV_NdogO5HotQhd-fDeXK81-xqNoT1uVPavJcz7hb6JNw%3D%3D)][[11](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQHkm0jCVQ2NCzgammbuMPWIUE6n_NvI97gtQa9cfeiHVcqDJ4dSbSDVjcnDl16xYewJwYWYPk35fsZEHRb064ffnDkjdhfsYTJ8zj_-3dmTrkgAZ4ew75L1be7C35Fn-rmlN5AUVlpxULE%3D)][[12](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGJ7MI451Gr27JL2ixKkO4iUuQIn4Z2rql0IOr9kqVme-GB9tbSoXJKg2N9KXqnaWme49iC_H1BFb0A-kt_AX4MW2nReNlbOqKiFFilkyc6S-rHpmc6URP0vaw54szgvZwoBu9eUqniKUFg)]. (e.g., a thermostat).
    
- **Model-Based Reflex Agents:** More advanced than simple reflex agents, these maintain an internal model of the world, allowing them to infer unobserved aspects of the current state and make decisions based on probable outcomes, not just immediate stimuli[[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGYqgrGZW76OCMxCtZzwapwrFQnAMxd6XElxwFED96_uH7KSzTdMC9LgdDLVj0h5dtzuBZd6SXAgJ5YDcDu53sUkH9RUckQwVWF3mnXhG7g5QYxsi34KHwNIXRDNQryRjkuP8g%3D)][[9](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGv6b5V0WrbdgEtzlGJRXOQTF91C2YEcGOLS6n5lf1KzIewUIM5vFBUGgat5UJIrEbDwvfp6rjxRDsCyixGMHWZiTJ6f2xyptE_LOa2j03DLQCwIjGp4EmYneYWHU-WnmE2DH7YRxytYmfJ18Dd_lV_NdogO5HotQhd-fDeXK81-xqNoT1uVPavJcz7hb6JNw%3D%3D)][[12](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGJ7MI451Gr27JL2ixKkO4iUuQIn4Z2rql0IOr9kqVme-GB9tbSoXJKg2N9KXqnaWme49iC_H1BFb0A-kt_AX4MW2nReNlbOqKiFFilkyc6S-rHpmc6URP0vaw54szgvZwoBu9eUqniKUFg)][[13](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFhts7oqh8IaePRoaCvR5w6xwjxrTSYDyOVJ-St-GHv-Wz8LsU2VauIziIYjutjgFwsaidSYvlS2NTPbSDdXD_ukJncuyRjBkLmGFSHTLu7c7_6di3BMDu9osQ7Eosp8T9pSNDdzkpSM_dlUN7ynfMmGOMGAyVQcXPzOzfs)].

- **Utility-Based Agents:** Employ complex reasoning to assist users in maximizing desired outcomes, comparing different scenarios and their respective "utility" or value[[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGYqgrGZW76OCMxCtZzwapwrFQnAMxd6XElxwFED96_uH7KSzTdMC9LgdDLVj0h5dtzuBZd6SXAgJ5YDcDu53sUkH9RUckQwVWF3mnXhG7g5QYxsi34KHwNIXRDNQryRjkuP8g%3D)][[11](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQHkm0jCVQ2NCzgammbuMPWIUE6n_NvI97gtQa9cfeiHVcqDJ4dSbSDVjcnDl16xYewJwYWYPk35fsZEHRb064ffnDkjdhfsYTJ8zj_-3dmTrkgAZ4ew75L1be7C35Fn-rmlN5AUVlpxULE%3D)][[12](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGJ7MI451Gr27JL2ixKkO4iUuQIn4Z2rql0IOr9kqVme-GB9tbSoXJKg2N9KXqnaWme49iC_H1BFb0A-kt_AX4MW2nReNlbOqKiFFilkyc6S-rHpmc6URP0vaw54szgvZwoBu9eUqniKUFg)].
    
- **Learning Agents:** These agents can continuously improve over time by learning from past interactions, adapting their behavior and decision-making processes based on feedback and experience[[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGYqgrGZW76OCMxCtZzwapwrFQnAMxd6XElxwFED96_uH7KSzTdMC9LgdDLVj0h5dtzuBZd6SXAgJ5YDcDu53sUkH9RUckQwVWF3mnXhG7g5QYxsi34KHwNIXRDNQryRjkuP8g%3D)][[9](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGv6b5V0WrbdgEtzlGJRXOQTF91C2YEcGOLS6n5lf1KzIewUIM5vFBUGgat5UJIrEbDwvfp6rjxRDsCyixGMHWZiTJ6f2xyptE_LOa2j03DLQCwIjGp4EmYneYWHU-WnmE2DH7YRxytYmfJ18Dd_lV_NdogO5HotQhd-fDeXK81-xqNoT1uVPavJcz7hb6JNw%3D%3D)].

    - **What:** Understand agents, environments, states, actions, rewards, and basic RL algorithms (e.g., Q-learning conceptually).
        
    - **How:** Online courses specializing in RL (e.g., from Coursera, Udacity). Start with conceptual understanding, then gradually move to basic implementations.
        
2. **Advanced Memory and Knowledge Representation:**
    
    - **Why:** Effective memory is crucial for agents to maintain context, learn, and avoid "forgetting"[[6](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFloFNfjjsgu_kpbDpYFfsILx6W1kam_0mHTlBAcM-TMZGkuwvWDIXD2XKQzRt1ttOoqkBtwbbVO9eh-QojqdYjNTuB_hdaBkR9LH19faut4Yta8U8iGO4i8wYvEceH1vI_Ar1xKcQ6)].
        
    - **What:** Explore vector databases, embeddings, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) for giving agents access to external, up-to-date information beyond their training data[[14](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQF_NEQxlzjt5430M-us6S1NXz6Hk-Jj_fH8gtNcPQn_G0E6UJUY8kcH0pQ4VBvvp-IMiECTCvEw5ArT-0AdqxZC52XW4kjIqjWXrkQpfQMMsNSIAPrIWoe1_hYbaVaaQqBojmd4fBtutTv4O0865JsBmnBjX-vDBoK-9UpEY0etkifQSk3ppww29p4cJqLD8Qch1yaDJi4PZi9k9z3qFpNJig%3D%3D)][[21](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGhUkfv2JE-Ra5G1DMHLBOPKYS42P3T95TN9mAJneH937gIwJnUN3zxOLn7Ou5hXxAbu40QXaqNi7TebO829gn_qckbGP8K8bLGWvKCDkgCpRv6xj6BQmK1vmaN1PDIoBHTOfA3yKFRkjsF5vTLUjctqtpHAg%3D%3D)].
        
    - **How:** Look for tutorials on RAG applications, vector databases (e.g., Pinecone, ChromaDB), and how they integrate with LLMs and agent frameworks.
        
3. **Coding with AI Agent Frameworks (Python-based):**
    
    - **Why:** For full flexibility, control, and performance, building agents with code is essential[[15](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFmNS7Tv25i-C1TCPkPN17yRdi-Sol0Uv74cwrsRBDrbdXQglweXDS3C8ZdxlLuX8Z20wKyCzjEdcAbMpAZfklGNlB8R947CRFb1d2juiVo3cpGW_d57TQHKmwIij6l4wSFiQqs-w%3D%3D)].
        
    - **What:** Get hands-on with popular Python frameworks like LangChain, Microsoft AutoGen, or CrewAI[[18](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQHKj7vbUowtoD7mCTGaPQuND9QxwWjpl4NyQNT2kOVGJ_0b1CPBKBjBtrYVV_Xdi8bFwtNVaPPC60Cjus38YgqUZTIvxQMxO4d_9gSrRqanAShtNCAKceZMUJg0bZ4Mf0CuppIggc3qsG_epMBo2Oib-oaZ2kOThTsT)][[22](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFsnroK7MXoivaVmAsL0nQ0a32-RR_vYgcrbU01XkJ1pTeUmFGMgAxmoR7EttEyGR2CFqjxcIYbDjbeUbcEwY71M6h86GSkn7vYv_mIgK26KhoXoDy4ycuFsULrpxadFTrVpErerwAbGyEiARJB)]. These help orchestrate LLMs, tools, and memory.
        
    - **How:** Follow official documentation and beginner tutorials for these frameworks. Start by reimplementing the simple agents you built with no-code tools.
        
4. **Multi-Agent Systems and Orchestration:**
    
    - **Why:** Many complex real-world problems require multiple specialized agents working together[[1](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFjMaISM7-aHnUEG8RQ02WjYeIa8_sGAhh_xewfKGn-FuxkX5C-axxx5b0jCo6yZZXf-XozUmFlSo5JuW_jjbl3NMdBhJ4mZowL1qh0gJlWWM_k1_3bmPBoBiw07nZLXgsZgUIfgjC-rHMOq5ngUw%3D%3D)][[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGYqgrGZW76OCMxCtZzwapwrFQnAMxd6XElxwFED96_uH7KSzTdMC9LgdDLVj0h5dtzuBZd6SXAgJ5YDcDu53sUkH9RUckQwVWF3mnXhG7g5QYxsi34KHwNIXRDNQryRjkuP8g%3D)][[21](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGhUkfv2JE-Ra5G1DMHLBOPKYS42P3T95TN9mAJneH937gIwJnUN3zxOLn7Ou5hXxAbu40QXaqNi7TebO829gn_qckbGP8K8bLGWvKCDkgCpRv6xj6BQmK1vmaN1PDIoBHTOfA3yKFRkjsF5vTLUjctqtpHAg%3D%3D)].
        
    - **What:** Learn how multiple agents can communicate, collaborate, or even compete to achieve a common objective[[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGYqgrGZW76OCMxCtZzwapwrFQnAMxd6XElxwFED96_uH7KSzTdMC9LgdDLVj0h5dtzuBZd6SXAgJ5YDcDu53sUkH9RUckQwVWF3mnXhG7g5QYxsi34KHwNIXRDNQryRjkuP8g%3D)][[9](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGv6b5V0WrbdgEtzlGJRXOQTF91C2YEcGOLS6n5lf1KzIewUIM5vFBUGgat5UJIrEbDwvfp6rjxRDsCyixGMHWZiTJ6f2xyptE_LOa2j03DLQCwIjGp4EmYneYWHU-WnmE2DH7YRxytYmfJ18Dd_lV_NdogO5HotQhd-fDeXK81-xqNoT1uVPavJcz7hb6JNw%3D%3D)]. Understand the role of "orchestrator" agents[[4](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQGYqgrGZW76OCMxCtZzwapwrFQnAMxd6XElxwFED96_uH7KSzTdMC9LgdDLVj0h5dtzuBZd6SXAgJ5YDcDu53sUkH9RUckQwVWF3mnXhG7g5QYxsi34KHwNIXRDNQryRjkuP8g%3D)].

5. **Evaluation, Monitoring, and Ethics:**
    
    - **Why:** Building responsible and effective agents requires careful evaluation, continuous monitoring, and consideration of ethical implications.
        
    - **What:** Understand concepts like hallucination, bias, safety, and how to measure agent performance.
        
    - **How:** Read articles on AI ethics and best practices for developing AI systems.

- **Learn by Doing:** The most effective way to learn is by building. Start with small, achievable projects and iterate[[16](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQEjt92hTjetAFzz7vuJzceSReU8l143SG7gc83hj-_VaQ4dbxnYasZwmU0t8K2GhEgIpqGTSkfg4RWmfTXdUgzCzy8MN0SZO1Vq4PoHJKlgmAQqO1gHI56cnWvKNvI7-G54KbENMSxxtL2JpnyHNqvxg3EaMT8gir_rrdK8td36GlfjYOv4jjZHXzhzkjlOKCZL3MEsgyumANgW2geirwFf6g%3D%3D)].

- **Focus on Capabilities, Not Just Tools:** Understand the underlying concepts (e.g., tool use, memory) rather than just mastering a specific framework that might evolve rapidly[[15](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&q=https%3A%2F%2Fvertexaisearch.cloud.google.com%2Fgrounding-api-redirect%2FAUZIYQFmNS7Tv25i-C1TCPkPN17yRdi-Sol0Uv74cwrsRBDrbdXQglweXDS3C8ZdxlLuX8Z20wKyCzjEdcAbMpAZfklGNlB8R947CRFb1d2juiVo3cpGW_d57TQHKmwIij6l4wSFiQqs-w%3D%3D)].
    
- **Stay Updated:** The field of AI agents is evolving rapidly, so continuously reading blogs, research papers (even summaries), and following key developers is important.
